The love for VolleyBall

Volleyball is a game that has been around for centuries. Just as any other sport, it is a game played by all gender and loved by all. Every game is made of two teams comprising of 6 members each except in certain situations where the number of players in a team may vary. The game is won in a three out of five sets consisting of twenty-five points each except the fifth set is consisted of fifteen points since it is the tie breaker.

Just like football and Basketball, VolleyBall is recognised and played all over the world, played in international and local competitional, high schools, streets etc.

VolleyBall games is a game that has brought alot of people together through the love of the game.


Here are some facts about VolleyBall

  1. A player will jump an average of 300 times per game
  2. Volleyball originally used a basketball untill 1900, when it was changed to a rubber by William G Morgan
  3. The game is regarded as the most popular sport in the world right after soccer
